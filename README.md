## 📋 Problem Statement

India has **15 million+ gig workers** (Zomato, Swiggy, Uber, Urban Company) who earn regularly but cannot access loans or credit cards. Traditional banks reject them because they lack salary slips, IT returns, or credit history. They are forced to borrow from local lenders at **36-60% annual interest**, trapping them in debt cycles.

**Scale of Impact:**
- 15M+ gig workers in India (NITI Aayog Report 2023)
- 90% excluded from formal credit
- ₹50,000 crore annual credit gap
- 23.5M projected gig workers by 2030

---

## 💡 Our Solution

**DigiCredit** is an AI-powered digital lending platform that uses alternative data—delivery history, customer ratings, completion rates, and app-based earnings—to generate a **"Dynamic Trust Score."** This score unlocks instant microloans directly to UPI ID, with repayments automatically deducted from future earnings.

### Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Alternative Credit Scoring** | ML algorithm analyzes gig platform data to predict creditworthiness |
| **Instant UPI Disbursement** | Loans sent to UPI ID within 2 minutes of approval |
| **Revenue-Based Repayment** | 5-10% auto-deducted per gig—adjusts with earnings, no fixed EMI |
| **No Paperwork** | Fully digital, no CIBIL score required |
